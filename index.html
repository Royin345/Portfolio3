Music & Culture

A DJ identity forged from passion, persistence, and a deep love for the underground. DJYou has been a cornerstone of Atlanta's rave community and the driving force behind Happy Hardcore's presence throughout the 2010s.
2009

The Beginning

Found an interest in DJing and fell in love with Happy Hardcore — a genre defined by speed, energy, and unapologetic joy.

Mar 2010

MomoCon — The Spark

Attended MomoCon armed with very loud computer speakers — much to everyone's surprise — and started an impromptu dance that would become the original precursor to MomoCon's Rave.

Sep 2010

Into the Underground

After high school, entered the underground rave scene shortly after returning from a first rave in Toronto. Began relentlessly pushing the Happy Hardcore genre throughout the underground Atlanta community.

Mar 2011

MomoCon — Bigger & Louder

Attempted another impromptu dance at MomoCon with much bigger and louder speakers — until the con chair came down personally to shut it down.

Mar 2012

MomoCon — Officially In Charge

MomoCon officially put DJYou in charge of the rave. What started as a rogue disruption became a recognized, curated event.

2012–2017

Director of MomoCon's Rave

Served as Director of MomoCon's Rave for five years, shaping it into a hallmark event of the convention. Throughout this entire era, Happy Hardcore was pushed relentlessly through the underground scene.

2017+

Closing Out the Rave

Stepped down from the Director role to focus solely on what matters most — closing out the rave every year. Ending the night is the honor.

The Full Picture

About

Ian Green is a coach, DJ, gamer, and community builder based in Atlanta, GA. Born October 31st, 1991, he has spent his life turning the things he loves into things that matter to the people around him.

As Head Coach at Atlanta Contact Point, Ian has led the PlayDay program for many years — a community athletic experience centered on inclusive, joyful play. He also occasionally hosts at Sweet Auburn Barbecue in Virginia Highlands, and works the spring season at Intown Ace Hardware, staying rooted in the neighborhood he calls home.

Under the name DJYou, Ian has been a fixture of Atlanta's underground rave scene since 2009 — championing Happy Hardcore throughout his twenties. What started as a rogue set of loud speakers at MomoCon 2010 grew into five years as Director of MomoCon's Rave (2012–2017). Today, he focuses on what he loves most: closing out the rave each year.

Off the decks and off the field, Ian is a dedicated gamer and PC enthusiast. His first build, Crystal, runs an i9-9900K and RTX 2070. His second build, Aether, is a high-end work in progress — a powerhouse featuring the Ryzen 9 9950X3D and RX 7900 XTX. He stays active through DDR, PlayDay, and regular exercise.
